Golden Temple

The Golden Temple (also known as the Harmandir Sahib) is a gurdwara located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India.It is the preeminent spiritual site of Sikhism.
The man-made pool on the site of the temple was completed by the fourth Sikh Guru, Guru Ram Das, in 1577.
The Golden Temple is an open house of worship for all people, from all walks of life and faiths.
